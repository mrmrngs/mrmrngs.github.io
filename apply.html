Apply for a Puppy

Thank you for your interest in a puppy from Nevena Havanese. Our goal is to find the right home for each of our puppies. Please complete every question on the form; your answers will help us make the right puppy-human match to ensure a future happy family.

We respond to all questions and inquiries promptly. If you do not hear back from us within 24 hours, please email again from a different account, or submit a contact form via the Contact page. Emails from some providers are more likely to be flagged as spam (even when legitimate), and then either blocked or put into the spam/junk folder.
