Our beautiful Havanese puppies descend from AKC champion lines, and come in parti colors such as chocolate (rare, and our specialty), black, and occasionally brindle, sable, and solid white. However, all Havanese dogs have the same wonderful personality and robust health, no matter their color!

It is very important to match the right puppy with its new family, and we are very particular about the homes into which our puppies are placed. The Havanese breed is a long-lived breed, and your puppy will be part of your family for about 15 years. Havanese dogs do not like to be left alone, and should be in a home where they will receive lots of attention and affection.

Our Care & Nurture Philosophy

Our Havanese puppies are raised in our family room, with plenty of love and attention. We believe the best life for our Havanese puppies is one with people that will love and cherish them. We do not consider our Havanese puppies to have separate "show" or "pet" qualities. They are all bred to be "outstanding" quality, with good health and sweet temperament. We prefer to place our Havanese puppies in forever homes as family members.

Puppy Health & Temperament

We strive first and foremost to breed for health and temperament, with focus on breed standard. We only breed extensively health-tested Havanese dogs who pass all health testing required by the Havanese Club Of America. We health screen our breeding Havanese dogs because everyone deserves a healthy puppy!
